Part 1 — QA Maturity Assessment

Executive Summary

The organization currently operates at a low QA maturity level, with fragmented ownership, inconsistent processes, unstable automation, and limited quality metrics. Based on a structured maturity model, the current state aligns with Level 1 (Initial) to early Level 2 (Managed).

Part 2 — 24-Month QA Maturity Roadmap

0–6 Months — Foundation

1. Establish QA Governance (QA Chapter)
2. Introduce Org-level QA OKRs
3. Standardize Processes (DoR/DoD, Test Strategy)
4. Automation Tooling PoC
5. Stabilize Test Data & Environments
6. Launch Quality Dashboard

6–12 Months — Scaling

1. Roll Out Unified Automation Framework
2. Implement CI/CD Quality Gates
3. Strengthen Shift-left Practices
4. Build Central Test Data Platform
5. QA Bootcamps & Developer Testing Training

12–24 Months — Optimization

1. Predictive Quality Analytics (TIA, flakiness prediction)
2. Model-based + Visual Regression Testing
3. Performance Benchmarking Pipelines
4. Quarterly QA Maturity Reviews
5. Quality Scorecards & Champions Program

End-State Vision

Optimized Quality Engineering Organization:
✔ Stable Automation
✔ Strong Governance
✔ Predictive Quality Insights
✔ Shared Quality Culture
✔ Fast, Reliable Releases
